About

First, select three points. They will be highlighted with red circles. After you set all three, you'll see a blue parallelogram, having three of its corners in the points. And yet a yellow circle, with the same area and the centre of mass as the parallelogram.

You'll see coordinates of the selected points as well as the area of the parallelogram and circle. You are free to move around the points.
